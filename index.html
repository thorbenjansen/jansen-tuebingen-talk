Slide 1

Effective Argumentation Instruction Across Science and Language Education

Supporting teachers' formative assessment, student motivation and learning with pedagogical AI.

Starting point

1
Importance

Argumentative competencies are central to participation in society

  • Argumentation supports building one's own position.
  • Argumentation competencies enable communication of one's own positions.
2
Complexity

Argumentation is a multifaceted competence.

  • Science: evidence, claims, and explanatory models have to fit the domain.
  • Language education: argument structure, position, and linguistic quality have to work together.
3
⚡
Problem

Teachers need time and process support

Effective instruction requires continuous diagnosis, feedback, and guidance while students are developing their arguments.

4
Solution

Digital tools and AI can support teachers and students

They can make learning visible in the process and help teachers provide timely formative support.

PISA writing study

Texts written with chatbots receive higher scores

Population sample The distributions compare texts written with and without chatbot support in the pre-sample.

Automated scoring during writing

Writing products can be scored reliably before students finish

Early signal Model quality rises quickly as more writing time becomes available.
Fusion helps most early The combined model performs best at short writing-time thresholds and remains strong at the full-text endpoint.
