## Content Summary

This systematic review and meta-analysis evaluates growth mindset interventions, focusing on heterogeneity in effects and moderators such as subsamples expected to benefit and implementation fidelity. It also proposes a process model for why and how interventions might work. This is a strong source for the wiki's motivation, self-beliefs, and intervention-quality cluster.

## Key Source-Checked Takeaways

- Growth mindset intervention effects should be understood through moderators and implementation fidelity.
- The paper is directly relevant to self-beliefs and student motivation.
- It helps avoid overgeneralizing mindset effects across all learners and settings.
