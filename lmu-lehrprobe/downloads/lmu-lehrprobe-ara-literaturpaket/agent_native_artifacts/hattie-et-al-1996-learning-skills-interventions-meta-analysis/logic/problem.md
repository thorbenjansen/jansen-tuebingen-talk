# Problem

## Research Problem

Meta-analysis of interventions intended to improve learning by teaching task-related, self-management, and affective learning skills. It is central for the wiki's learning-process layer because it distinguishes simple skill training from situated, active, metacognitively aware strategy use.

## Wiki Role

This source strengthens the database by connecting empirical synthesis evidence to concept pages rather than by acting as an isolated paper note.

## Why It Matters

- Learning-skills interventions are strongest when they are situated in meaningful content and tasks, rather than taught as isolated generic techniques.
- Metacognitive awareness and active learner engagement are important conditions for transfer beyond simple mnemonic performance.
