# Methods, Variables, and Outcomes

## Method Profile

- **Source type:** Meta-analysis with additional PISA analysis
- **Evidence base:** Studies reporting relations among self-concept, self-efficacy, and academic achievement
- **Outcomes:** Discriminant validity and incremental prediction of achievement

## Outcome Or Contribution

Huang synthesizes studies on the discriminant and incremental validity of academic self-concept and self-efficacy, and also analyses PISA data. The source is valuable because it prevents the wiki from collapsing all self-beliefs into one construct. It supports careful separation between domain-specific self-concept, task-focused self-efficacy, and achievement prediction.

## Limits

Use for construct clarification; exact moderator/results details should be checked for manuscript claims.
