# Limitations Passage Summary

Important limitations:

- Broad aggregation of self-belief constructs and domains.
- Predominance of correlational evidence.
- Shared method variance among self-report variables.
- Some mixed K-12/non-K-12 samples included.
- Weighting can give strong influence to large meta-analyses.
- Limited ability to test moderators across categories.
- Cultural and language bias in the evidence base.
- Between-person effects do not establish within-person mechanisms.

Source location: pp. 1307-1309.
