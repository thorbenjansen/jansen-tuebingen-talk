# Experiments / Evidence Design

This source is not an experiment. It is a second-order meta-analytic review.

## Evidence Design

- Unit of synthesis: first-order meta-analytic effect sizes.
- Included evidence: 105 first-order meta-analyses, 493 first-order effect sizes, more than 8,500 primary studies, more than 16 million children/adolescents.
- Analysis: second-order random-effects meta-analysis with robust variance estimation.
- Effect metric: standardized mean difference (SMD).
- Quality/risk-of-bias layer: transparency and reproducibility criteria adapted from Polanin et al. (2020).

## Verification Checks for Agents

1. Check whether a later claim refers to observational associations or intervention effects.
2. Preserve the causal boundary: most results are descriptive associations.
3. When using numeric results, cite the relevant table:
   - Table 3 for personal characteristics versus interventions.
   - Table 4 for personal-characteristic categories.
   - Table 5 for personal-characteristic subcategories.
   - Tables 6 and 7 for intervention categories and subcategories.
4. For methodological transparency claims, use Figure 3 and the risk-of-bias discussion.
