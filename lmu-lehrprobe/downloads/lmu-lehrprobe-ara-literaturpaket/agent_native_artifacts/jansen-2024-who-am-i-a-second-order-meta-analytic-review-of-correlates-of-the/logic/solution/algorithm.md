# Algorithm / Analytic Procedure

1. Search for first-order meta-analyses on self-belief constructs and their correlates in K-12 children/adolescents.
2. Screen abstracts and full texts using inclusion criteria.
3. Double-code eligible meta-analyses.
4. Convert first-order effect sizes to standardized mean differences.
5. Categorize variables into personal characteristics, interventions, and situational variables.
6. Compute second-order random-effects meta-analyses.
7. Use robust variance estimation to account for dependent effect sizes.
8. Adjust weights for primary-study overlap where possible.
9. Conduct moderator analyses and influence checks.
10. Evaluate transparency and reproducibility criteria.
