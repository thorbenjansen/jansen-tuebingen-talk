## Content Summary

Meta-analysis of classroom management programs and strategies in primary education. It connects teacher-controlled learning environments to academic, behavioral, social-emotional, and motivational outcomes, making it useful for the teacher/context layer of formative assessment and AI-supported classroom writing.
