# Experiments

This source is a review/meta-analysis rather than a primary experiment.

## Evidence Type

- Design: Meta-analysis of controlled classroom-management intervention studies.
- Source material: 54 randomized and nonrandomized controlled intervention studies from 2003-2013 in primary education.

## Experimental Detail Policy

Use `logic/method_and_design.md` and `logic/methods_variables_outcomes.md` for the review design. Do not infer primary experimental procedures beyond what the review reports.
