# Method And Design

## Design Type

Meta-analysis of controlled classroom-management intervention studies.

## Source Material

54 randomized and nonrandomized controlled intervention studies from 2003-2013 in primary education.

## Method Workflow

- Synthesizes effects across academic, behavioral, social-emotional, and motivational outcomes.
- Codes programs by focus on teacher behavior, student behavior, students' social-emotional development, and teacher-student relationships.
- Examines which strategy categories contribute to effectiveness.

## Population Or Study Context

The population and context are inherited from the included studies. For synthesis, use this ARA as review/meta-analytic evidence rather than as a single primary classroom sample.

## Citation-Grade Verification Needed

- Verify exact inclusion counts, effect sizes, and moderator coefficients in the PDF before quoting them.
- Use the source PDF and raw Markdown sidecar for exact statistical reporting.
