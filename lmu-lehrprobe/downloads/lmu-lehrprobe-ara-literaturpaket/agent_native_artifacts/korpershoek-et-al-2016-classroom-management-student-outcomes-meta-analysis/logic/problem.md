# Problem

## Research Problem

Meta-analysis of classroom management programs and strategies in primary education. It connects teacher-controlled learning environments to academic, behavioral, social-emotional, and motivational outcomes, making it useful for the teacher/context layer of formative assessment and AI-supported classroom writing.

## Wiki Role

This source strengthens the database by connecting empirical synthesis evidence to concept pages rather than by acting as an isolated paper note.

## Why It Matters

- Classroom management interventions show small but meaningful effects across student outcomes, with weaker evidence for motivational outcomes.
- Programs focused on students' social-emotional development contribute especially to social-emotional outcomes.
