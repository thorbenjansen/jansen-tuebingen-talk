# Theories

## Theory Inventory

### Classroom Management

- Theory concept: [[Classroom Management]]
- Role in this source: Frames classroom organization, preventive routines, relationships, and behavior supports as conditions for learning.
- How the source uses it: Central construct and intervention family.
- APA anchor: Korpershoek et al. (2016).

### Teacher-Student Relationships

- Theory concept: [[Teacher-Student Relationships]]
- Role in this source: Positive relationships are treated as a classroom-management strategy and a condition for effective education.
- How the source uses it: Used as one coded intervention category.
- APA anchor: Korpershoek et al. (2016), building on Wubbels and Marzano traditions.

### Social-Emotional Learning

- Theory concept: [[Social-Emotional Learning]]
- Role in this source: Student social-emotional development is treated as a pathway through which management programs affect outcomes.
- How the source uses it: Used as a moderator/component category.
- APA anchor: Korpershoek et al. (2016).

## APA Reference For This Source

Korpershoek, H., Harms, T., de Boer, H., van Kuijk, M., & Doolaard, S. (2016). A meta-analysis of the effects of classroom management strategies and classroom management programs on students' academic, behavioral, emotional, and motivational outcomes. Review of Educational Research, 86(3), 643-680. https://doi.org/10.3102/0034654315626799
