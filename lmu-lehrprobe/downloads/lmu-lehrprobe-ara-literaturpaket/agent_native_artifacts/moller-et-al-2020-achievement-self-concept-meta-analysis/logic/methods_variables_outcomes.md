# Methods, Variables, and Outcomes

## Method Profile

- **Source type:** Meta-analysis
- **Included evidence:** 505 datasets containing bivariate correlations among achievement and self-concepts
- **Constructs:** Achievement and self-concept across school subjects
- **Moderator interest:** Subject domains, teacher grades versus standardized tests, dimensional comparisons

## Outcome Or Contribution

Moeller et al. update and broaden meta-analytic evidence on relations between achievement and academic self-concept. Building on the internal/external frame of reference model, the paper synthesizes many datasets across subject combinations. It is important for the wiki because teacher grades, standardized tests, and subject domains can shape students' self-concepts differently.

## Limits

Broad achievement/self-concept evidence; not a writing-specific intervention.
