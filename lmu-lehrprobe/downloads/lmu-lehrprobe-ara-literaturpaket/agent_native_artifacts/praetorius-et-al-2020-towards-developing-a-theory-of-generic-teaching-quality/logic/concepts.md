# Concepts

- [[Teachers]] - Teaching quality is treated as a property of teacher instructional practice and its consequences for students.
- [[Diagnostic Competence]] - The model is relevant to how teacher practice is described, observed, and interpreted.
- [[Formative Assessment]] - Student support and cognitive activation overlap with feedback, scaffolding, and adaptive instruction.
- [[Assessment And Formative Assessment]] - The paper is useful as a theory anchor for quality criteria around instruction, feedback, and classroom support.
- [[Learning Processes]] - The proposed mechanisms connect teaching dimensions to student time on task, motivation-related experience, and depth of processing.
