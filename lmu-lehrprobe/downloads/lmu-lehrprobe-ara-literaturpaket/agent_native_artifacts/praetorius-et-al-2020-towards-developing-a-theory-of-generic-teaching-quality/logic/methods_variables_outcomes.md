# Methods, Variables, Outcomes

## Variables Or Constructs

- Classroom management
- Student support
- Cognitive activation
- Teaching quality
- Student time on task
- Student autonomy, competence, and social relatedness
- Student depth of processing

## Outcomes

- Conceptual outcome: clearer theory of generic teaching quality.
- Empirical implication: future studies should test mediating processes and relations among teaching-quality dimensions more explicitly.

## Boundaries

- This ARA should not be used as evidence that a specific intervention improves achievement.
- It is best retrieved as an upstream theory/model source for teaching quality, instructional support, and construct definition.
