# Theories

- Three Basic Dimensions Model: classroom management, student support, and cognitive activation as generic dimensions of teaching quality.
- Self-Determination Theory: cited as a theoretical foundation for student support through autonomy, competence, and relatedness.
- Constructivist/cognitive activation accounts: used as theoretical background for challenging tasks, questions, discourse, and depth of processing.
- Theory of instruction criteria: used as an evaluative lens for deciding what the Three Basic Dimensions model lacks as a theory.
